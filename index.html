Bridging Theory & Practice

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We emphasise the powerful link between deep academic insight—through our UK university partnership—and hands-on, real-world projects. Our mission is to ensure that the next generation of risk professionals is prepared for the rapid shifts in digital landscapes and AI governance.

Who We Serve

Our community consists of students, early-career professionals, and seasoned practitioners seeking upskilling in digital and cyber risk.

We specifically focus on critical sectors like financial services and national infrastructure, where digital risk is not just a technical issue, but a core strategic challenge. IDR provides the bridge for those moving into regulated, high-consequence technology environments.
